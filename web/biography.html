BIOGRAPHY

Ado

Singer / Songwriter

Ado (アド, born October 24, 2002) is a 22-year-old Japanese singer, debuted in 2020 with "Usseewa," achieving massive success. Her 2022 album Kyogen and songs for ONE PIECE FILM RED became chart-topping hits. In 2024, she launched her world tour, performed at Japan's National Stadium, and announced her second album Zanmu for release in July.
