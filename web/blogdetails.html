"Mona Lisa in Profile" has ended.

The “Profile of Mona Lisa” tour marked a transformative chapter in my journey,blending personal growth with creative exploration. From debuting on stage with aguitar to embracing new challenges like dance, every moment was a step toward self-discovery.

Collaborations with talented artists and heartfelt connections with the audience made this experience unforgettable. Though this chapter has come to an end, the memories and lessons will inspire future endeavors. Thank you for being part of this meaningful journey—I look forward to sharing even more with you in the future.

Closing a Chapter with Gratitude

The “Profile of Mona Lisa” tour concluded with heartfelt performances, including my first guitar debut. Though health issues delayed the Nagoya show, your patience and support filled me with joy and relief.

Inspiration and Self-Expression

Covering Kessoku Band's “That Band” during the encore revived my high school guitar dreams. While imperfect, this performance brought deep personal fulfillment and reconnected me with my creative roots.

Appreciation for Phantom Cita

Thank you, Phantom Cita, for opening the tour with such dedication and growth. Watching your journey was inspiring, and I'm excited to see your bright future unfold.

New Challenges and Future Goals

Realizing my chair dance dream was a milestone, despite its amateur execution. This tour was a step toward self-acceptance, and I’m eager to continue growing and sharing my journey with you.
